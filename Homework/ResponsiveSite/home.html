Mercedes Benz GLB 35 AMG

The Mercedes GLB is an entire new model for 2020. For 2021 we will be seeing the AMG version being introduced. This new model will have a 2.0L 4 Cyclinder engine producing 306 horsepower, AMG suspension, AMG steering and exterior styling make the car much sportier than its cheapier counterpart, the GLB 250.

2020 Chevrolet Corvette

The all New 2020 Corvette was completley redone from the ground up. This years Corvette comes with a 6.2L Mid Engine V8 motor with 490 HP and 460 pound feet of torque. It has a 0-60 time of 2.9 seconds which is class leading.

2020 Porsche Macan GTS

The Porsche Macan GTS has been around by 2016, but for the 2020 model year the have decided to give it a midcycle refresh. There are new LED Lights on the back with an LED light bar. The infotainment on the inside has also been upgraded to a much bigger scream. The 3.0L V6 Engine has increased from 360HP to 375HP for the new model year.

2020 Lamborghini Huracan
