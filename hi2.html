2020 Porsche Taycan Turbo S

The Porsche Taycan is an entire new model for 2020. This is a bold move for Porsche, as it is their first fully electric car. The car makes an impressive 750 Horsepower when using launch control and has a range of around 192 miles on the Turbo S varient. The styling looks like a blend of the Porsche 911 and the Panamera.

2020 Chevrolet Corvette

The all New 2020 Corvette was completley redone from the ground up. This years Corvette comes with a 6.2L Mid Engine V8 motor with 490 HP and 460 pound feet of torque. It has a 0-60 time of 2.9 seconds which is class leading.

2020 M8 competetion

The Bmw M8 is Bmw's new flagship model. Coming in two varients, the m870i and the M8 version. The M8 competetion is an extension of the M8 model producing 617 HP and 553 pound of torque. This beautiful piece of technology also reaches 0-60 in a whopping 3.1 seconds.

2020 Lamborghini Huracan Evo

The Lmaborghihi Huracan Evo was a new model for 2019. The Huracan Evo comes after the orgiinal Huracan and the Huracan Performante.The Huracan continues on its legacy as V10 Flagship from Lamborghini
